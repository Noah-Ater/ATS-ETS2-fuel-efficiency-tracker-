A single-file offline-first fuel efficiency tracker for American Truck Simulator and Euro Truck Simulator 2.

Features

MPG tracking for ATS, L/100km tracking for ETS2
Cargo weight input with lbs/tons conversion (ATS) and ton-mile efficiency
Fleet tagging and filtering by truck model
Trend chart, KPI strip, and job history log
CSV export and import for data backup and transfer
All data stored locally in your browser — nothing leaves your machine

Usage

Open the app in your browser. No installation, no server, no internet connection required after the first load.
